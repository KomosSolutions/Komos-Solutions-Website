Use of Cookies

Like most websites, our Websites use cookies and similar technology. Cookies are small text files that are sent to your computer when you use a website. A cookie only tracks which computer is logging on to the Websites and tracks the actions and the pages visited on our Websites, it does not necessarily identify who is using that computer.

If you continue to use the Websites without consenting to the use of cookies or otherwise having declined their use, we will not send any cookies to your computer. In this case or if you disable or delete cookies through your browser, you will not be able to use all the functionality of the Websites.

The Websites use both persistent and session cookies. Persistent cookies are cookies that stay on your computer permanently until you 'manually' delete them. Session cookies delete themselves automatically when you leave the Website and go to another one or shut down your browser.
